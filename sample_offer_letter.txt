EMPLOYMENT OFFER LETTER

Dear Candidate,

We are pleased to offer you the position of Software Engineer at TechCorp Pvt. Ltd.

COMPENSATION:
Your gross salary will be INR 8,00,000 per annum (CTC), paid monthly.

PROBATION PERIOD:
You will be on a probation period of 6 months from the date of joining. Confirmation is subject to performance review.

LEAVE POLICY:
You are entitled to 18 days of annual leave, 7 days of sick leave, and 5 days of casual leave per year.

NON-COMPETE CLAUSE:
You agree that during your employment and for a period of 2 years after leaving the company, you shall not compete with TechCorp by joining any competitor or starting a business in the same domain.

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY:
All inventions, work product, code, and ideas created during your employment — including personal projects and side projects — shall vest entirely with TechCorp. The company owns all intellectual property developed by you during this period.

TERMINATION:
The company reserves the right to terminate immediately without notice if performance standards are not met or any policy violation is detected.

ARBITRATION:
Any disputes arising out of this agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration only. You waive your right to court proceedings or class action suits.

CONFIDENTIALITY:
You agree to maintain strict confidentiality of all trade secrets, proprietary information, and business strategies. This obligation continues even after your employment ends.

NOTICE PERIOD:
You are required to serve a notice period of 60 days before resignation. The company may waive this at its discretion.

BONUS:
Any bonus or variable pay is at the discretion of management and is not guaranteed as part of your compensation.

By signing below, you agree to all terms and conditions stated in this offer letter.

TechCorp Pvt. Ltd.
